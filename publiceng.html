Public Engagement


In addition to our research, we are committed to sharing knowledge and engaging with the community in crop sciences. We organize and participate in workshops, webinars, and seminars, working with students, farmers, schools, and industry partners to promote crop improvement, sustainable farming practices, and agricultural education. Through these activities, we aim to connect scientific research with real-world applications and help people better understand how our work supports productive, resilient, and sustainable cropping systems.
